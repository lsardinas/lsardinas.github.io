We make therapy work

Leloh Therapy is committed to providing flexibility in accessing our services. We accept insurance plans from employers, making it more convenient for you to prioritize your mental health within your existing coverage.
We believe in making mental health services accessible, our session fees are $140 per session, and this includes the applicable HST.
We offer sliding scale options. This means that we strive to accommodate various financial circumstances, ensuring that quality mental health support is available to everyone.
